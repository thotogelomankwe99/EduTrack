About EduTrack Gauteng

EduTrack Gauteng is a digital attendance management system designed for secondary schools. We address the common challenges faced by educational institutions in tracking attendance efficiently.

Our platform replaces outdated paper-based systems with an efficient QR code solution that simplifies attendance tracking for both teachers and administrators.

With EduTrack Gauteng, schools can focus more on education and less on administrative tasks, ensuring accurate records and streamlined processes.

Challenges in School Attendance Tracking

Traditional attendance methods create significant administrative burdens and inaccuracies in secondary education systems.

Time-Consuming Manual Processes

Teachers spend valuable instructional time manually recording attendance in registers, taking away from teaching activities.

Inaccurate Records

Paper-based systems are prone to errors, lost records, and difficulties in tracking patterns of absenteeism.

Compliance Challenges

Meeting reporting requirements is cumbersome with manual systems, risking compliance issues.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about EduTrack Gauteng for secondary schools

What if students don't have smartphones?

No student smartphone is required. Schools can use existing tablets or teachers can use their own devices to scan student QR codes. We also provide a manual entry option for any situation where QR scanning isn't possible.

How does this help with reporting requirements?

Our system automatically generates reports in the exact formats required by education departments, making it easier for schools to comply with reporting requirements.

Is training provided for teachers?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training sessions for teachers and administrative staff, both in-person and through online resources, to ensure smooth implementation.

Can the system work offline?

Yes, the system can work offline with data syncing when connectivity is available. This is particularly important for schools in areas with intermittent internet access.
